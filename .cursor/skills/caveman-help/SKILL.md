---
name: caveman-help
description: >
  Quick-reference card for caveman levels, commands, and activation toggles.
---

When invoked, print only a compact help card:
- levels: `lite`, `full`, `ultra`, `wenyan-lite`, `wenyan-full`, `wenyan-ultra`
- enable: `/caveman` (or specific level command)
- disable: `/caveman-off`, `stop caveman`, or `normal mode`
- related: `/caveman-commit`, `/caveman-review`, `/caveman-compress`
