---
name: neandercode-help
description: >
  Quick-reference card for neandercode levels, commands, and activation toggles.
---

When invoked, print only a compact help card:
- levels: `lite`, `full`, `ultra`, `wenyan-lite`, `wenyan-full`, `wenyan-ultra`
- enable: `/neandercode` (or specific level command)
- disable: `/neandercode-off`, `stop neandercode`, or `normal mode`
- related: `/neandercode-commit`, `/neandercode-review`, `/neandercode-compress`
